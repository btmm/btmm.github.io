Building an Online Marketplace

We started with an idea for an e-commerce platform allowing fashion retailers an easy way to bring their business online. The app would make it easy for customers to shop for local fashion in an online marketplace. The site would allow users to "check" apparel they want to save and share with others.

Getting it Down on Paper

After working with the team to define their brand vision, we started developing the idea with the logo. Through many sketches, the logo we decided on was simple and conveys the core concept of the site with a check button.

The Home Page

The completed home page design of the app. The page was designed to welcome new users and inform them about how the site works. Local outlets and fashion are featured so users can get right to shopping.

Social Integration

The app was designed with social media in mind from the beginning. Here you can see the live Instagram feed featured on the home page.

Store Administration

This administration dashboard gives store owners an overview of sales and key metrics. The dashboard was designed to be familiar and easy to use.

Orders Section

The store orders would appear on this page allowing owners to begin fulfillment. Orders could be shipped or picked up by the customer.
